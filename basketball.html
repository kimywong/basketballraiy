Core Skills

Shooting

Learn the B.E.E.F. technique: Balance, Eyes, Elbow, and Follow-through. Perfecting your form is the first step to becoming a sharpshooter.

Ball Handling

Keep your head up and your dribble low. Master crossovers, between-the-legs, and behind-the-back moves to blow past defenders.

Defense

Stay in a low stance, slide your feet, and never reach. Great defense wins championships by creating pressure and turnovers.

Pro Training Tip: The Mikan Drill

To develop touch around the rim, practice the Mikan Drill. Stand under the hoop and alternate making layups with your left and right hand without letting the ball hit the floor. It builds coordination and finishing skills.
